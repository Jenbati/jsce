Manuscript Submission Guidelines

Welcome to the Journal of Science & Engineering (JScE) submission portal. Please follow the instructions below.

Full Instructions to Authors »

Step 1. Prepare Your Manuscript

  1. Download and use our official MS Word template.
  2. Ensure the manuscript includes: Title, Abstract (< 250 words), Keywords, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, References.
  3. Figures must be embedded or submitted as separate high-resolution files.

Step 2. Online Submission

Please prepare the following files before submitting:

  • Main manuscript (DOCX or PDF)
  • High-resolution figures (JPEG, PNG, TIFF)
  • Supplementary materials (if any)
Submit Manuscript Online »

Step 3. Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo a thorough peer-review process. Estimated timeline:

  • Initial editorial screening: 1–2 weeks
  • Peer review: 4–6 weeks
  • Decision communicated via email

Step 4. Publication

Accepted papers will be published online. You will receive page proofs for final approval before publication.

For inquiries, contact: editor@jsce.com.np
